## Testing

<i class="fa fa-check-square-o"></i> All specs are passing

<i class="fa fa-check-square-o"></i> New specs are well written, uses `let` helpers and `before` blocks

<i class="fa fa-check-square-o"></i> If Cucumber scenarios are provided in the ticket then they written, implemented and pass


## Rails specific

<i class="fa fa-check-square-o"></i> Views have minimal logic, encourage the use of helpers

<i class="fa fa-check-square-o"></i> Controller actions should strive to be simple

<i class="fa fa-check-square-o"></i> Methods are object oriented, not excessive, and in the right models

<i class="fa fa-check-square-o"></i> No inline styling
